## What is the Pocket Mirror app, and how can I use the app?

The Pocket Mirror app turns your smartphone into a digital mirror, which you can use to check your look quickly and easily. The app offers you many useful features:

- Enlarge the preview of the front camera. With this practical zoom, you can discover every detail.

- Adjust the brightness of the display to your environment.

- Your pocket mirror can be used in light or dark mode. So it adapts optimally to your needs.

After downloading you can use the app immediately. No registration is required.

The app does not collect or process any personal data. No usage data is collected either.

The app does not contain any advertising.

## Can I build and run the Pocket Mirror app on my own computer and device?

Yes, you can. Pocket Mirror is based on React Native and Expo. You can checkout this repo and run `npm i` followed by `npx expo start`. To find out more about Expo and the development process checkout [https://docs.expo.dev](https://docs.expo.dev).
